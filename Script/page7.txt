#TITLE#=From miner's diary, part six.
<END_FORMAT>

They took me to the infirmary to take a rest. The next day their abbot came to see me. He asked about the mines. I told him everything. Strange thing is he wasn't surprised, but when I told him about the boss his face turned into a mask of an old, tired man.
"So he has found us," he said to himself.
